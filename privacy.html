🌱 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sustainable Life ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

🔒 Privacy-First Approach: Your carbon footprint data is stored locally on your device by default. We believe in giving you full control over your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

When you use Sustainable Life, you may provide the following information:

  • Activity Data: Transportation methods, food choices, shopping habits, and energy usage
  • Location Data: Optional location information for tracking transportation activities
  • Health Data: Optional workout and exercise data from Apple Health
  • Profile Information: Daily CO₂ goals, unit preferences, and achievement data

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

We automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (e.g., features used, session duration)
  • Device Information: Device model, iOS version, and app version
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and performance metrics (if enabled)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: Calculate your carbon footprint and track your sustainability journey
  • Personalization: Provide customized insights and recommendations
  • Analytics: Understand app usage and improve user experience
  • Communication: Send you updates and notifications (with your consent)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage (Default)

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's CoreData framework. This means your carbon footprint data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

4.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your data will be encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account. This allows you to sync your data across multiple devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

4.3 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit (iCloud sync)
  • Local data protection using iOS sandboxing
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Regular security updates

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

We may disclose your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with applicable laws or legal processes
  • Service Providers: Limited access to trusted service providers (e.g., iCloud for storage)

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Delete your data at any time
  • Export: Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable optional features like location tracking or health data integration

7. Children's Privacy

Sustainable Life is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Data Transfers

If you enable iCloud sync, your data may be transferred to and stored on Apple's servers located in various countries. Apple's iCloud service complies with international data protection regulations.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
